Sri Gurindapalli Isaac Dayardhana Rao garu was born in Nagaram, Repalle district on December 11,<br>
1945, to Mr. Gurindapalli David garu and Mrs.Gurindapalli Devamani garu.Both of his parents were teachers

Sri.Gurindapalli Isaac Dayardhana Rao garu started teaching Sunday school when he was in his thirties and continued until the last day of his life.<br>
He loved the little ones very much and would be humble enough to use various teaching aids, like action songs, play acting and mime, in order to<br>
catch and keep the attention of the little kids.The little ones in turn thoroughly enjoyed his story-telling skills, voice modulation,songs and actions <br>
that aptly described the scenario to the kids. Often, his students could be heard, laughing and taking part in the play-acting part of the narration,<br>
because, Mr.G.I.Dayardhana Rao garu had the talent to make even the most shy and bashful little kid to loosen up and actively take part in the <br>
